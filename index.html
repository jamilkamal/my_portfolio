Our Activities

Kayak

For lovers of this kind of fun sport, we suggest you kayak on the waters of the rivers running or on the calm waters of the lake, with the possibility of catching soft fish, and especially trout.

For mountaineering enthusiasts and mountain sports, we offer you diverse activities full of challenge and adventure, climbing the highest peaks of the Atlas Mountains, and accessing multiple caves.

Mountain Bike

For mountain biking enthusiasts and adventure lovers, we suggest various trails in the depths of the attractive Atlas Mountains to enjoy its beautiful scenery and vast forests. This adventure is both challenging and fun.

Swiming
