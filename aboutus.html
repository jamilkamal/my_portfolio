About Us

Our History

Launched in 2018, Travel And Tasting has quickly established itself as a travel icon in Morocco. With its unique brand of traditional cuisine not found anywhere else.And it enjoys its strategic location in the center of Morocco in the middle of the Atlas Mountains with these vast forests, different lakes and waterfalls. We receive our visitors with great pleasure and we share with them all that nature has provided us.

All our guests are very satisfied with all that we present for them, and want to come back a second time.

Leadership

Moha Abdo President Of The Association

Based on his many experiences and travels around the world, he decided to establish a tourist association non-profit to spread the culture and traditions of the country, spread the spirit of tolerance and fraternity, exchange cultures among peoples, and reject violence and extremism.

Sato Fadi Secretary of the Association

Our CFO, Danny, as he is affectionately referred to by his colleagues, comes from a long established family tradition in farming and produce. His experiences growing up on a farm in the Australian outback gave him great appreciation for varieties of food sources. As he puts it in his own words, Everything that runs, wins, and everything that stays, pays!

Nimo Ahmed Association Clerk

Blessed with the most discerning gustatory sense, Agumbe, our CTO, personally ensures that every dish that we serve meets his exacting tastes. Our chefs dread the tongue lashing that ensues if their dish does not meet his exacting standards. He lives by his motto, You click only if you survive my lick.

Farah Ramzi Association Advisor

Award winning three-star Michelin chef with wide International experience having worked closely with whos-who in the culinary world, he specializes in creating mouthwatering Indo-Italian fusion experiences. He says, Put together the cuisines from the two craziest cultures, and you get a winning hit! Amma Mia!
